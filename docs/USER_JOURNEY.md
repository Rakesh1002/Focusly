## Journey To Validate

This journey covers the fake-door and prototype validation flow only. It is not a full product launch journey.

---

## Stage 1 - Trigger

The user recently lost a work session:

- opened the laptop and could not start,
- drifted into Slack/X/YouTube/research,
- forgot what they meant to do,
- returned later with no re-entry context.

Validation question:

> Is this painful enough that they will try a new Mac utility?

---

## Stage 2 - Landing Page

The user sees:

- "A Mac focus anchor that nudges you back when you drift"
- short explanation of task anchor, drift nudge, and re-entry note,
- privacy promise: no screenshots, no keystrokes, opt-in app/window detection,
- CTA to join beta or reserve early access.

Validation question:

> Does the message create qualified signups, or only generic curiosity?

---

## Stage 3 - Interview / Beta Application

The user answers:

- role,
- Mac usage,
- biggest focus pain,
- tools tried,
- monitoring comfort,
- willingness to pay.

Validation question:

> Are they the intended buyer and do they describe the problem in their own words?

---

## Stage 4 - Prototype First Session

If a prototype is available, the user:

1. enters one task,
2. starts a visible focus anchor,
3. sees a manual or heuristic drift nudge,
4. ends with a re-entry note.

Validation question:

> Does the loop feel useful in under 10 seconds, or does setup friction kill it?

---

## Stage 5 - Commitment

The user is asked for one concrete commitment:

- join a beta cohort,
- schedule a follow-up,
- pay/refundably reserve early access,
- introduce another qualified user.

Validation question:

> Does interest turn into commitment?

---

## Go / No-Go Readout

Go if:

- 10 interviews are completed,
- 200 qualified signups or 20 paid/beta commitments are reached,
- 70% of interviewees accept opt-in drift detection,
- users prefer the concept over obvious alternatives often enough to justify build.

No-Go if:

- signups are low-intent,
- privacy objections dominate,
- users prefer Focana/Focusmo/Focura/Focarium/Nudge,
- the app code cannot be found or built and greenfield is not approved.
