# Focusly - Validation ICP

**Date:** 24 May 2026  
**Owner:** [STRATEGY.md](../STRATEGY.md)  
**Purpose:** Define who to interview and recruit during the 2-week validation sprint.

---

## Primary Validation Audience

**Late-diagnosed ADHD or ADHD-aware Mac knowledge workers.**

This is a validation audience, not yet a proven commercial ICP.

### Required traits

- Uses a Mac as the primary work machine.
- Does knowledge work at least 20 hours/week.
- Has recurring problems with task initiation, mid-session drift, time blindness, or re-entry.
- Has tried at least one focus/productivity tool and lapsed.
- Is willing to discuss privacy boundaries around active app/window detection.

### Preferred roles

- PM
- designer
- engineer
- founder/operator
- consultant
- writer
- marketer
- academic/researcher

### Screening questions

1. Is your Mac your primary work device?
2. What kind of work do you do most weeks?
3. When did you last lose the thread during a work session?
4. What tools have you tried for focus or ADHD productivity?
5. Would an app that sees active app/window names feel acceptable if opt-in?
6. Would you pay for a Mac utility that reliably helped with this?

---

## Secondary Validation Audience

**Founder/operator with ADHD-coded focus patterns.**

This audience overlaps with the AI-builder fallback in [../PIVOT_A.md](../PIVOT_A.md). Include 2 of these users in interviews to see whether the sharper buyer is actually the founder/operator segment rather than the broader late-diagnosed ADHD segment.

Required traits:

- runs multiple projects or clients,
- works on a Mac,
- loses time to context switching,
- already pays for productivity or AI tools.

---

## Advisor, Not Buyer Proxy

**ADHD coach.**

Include at most 1 coach during validation. Coaches can explain client patterns and language, but they should not be treated as proof of B2C demand.

Do not build coach dashboards, seat management, client analytics, or affiliate programs during validation.

---

## Anti-ICP For Validation

Do not recruit these users for primary evidence:

- students under 18,
- iOS-only users,
- Windows-only users,
- people seeking medical treatment,
- generic Pomodoro users without ADHD-aware pain,
- enterprise buyers,
- coaches who are not also target end users.

---

## What Counts As A Qualified Signup

A signup is qualified only if the user:

- uses Mac for work,
- does knowledge work regularly,
- names a specific start/drift/re-entry pain,
- opts into beta/interview follow-up,
- indicates willingness to try or pay if the product works.

Raw email count is not enough.
