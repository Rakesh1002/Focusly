# Focusly - Pivot A / Builder Wedge

**Date:** 24 May 2026  
**Status:** Fallback pivot, not active  
**Owner:** [STRATEGY.md](./STRATEGY.md)

---

## Current Role Of This Doc

The active decision is **not** to commit to the full ADHD-Mac pivot. The approved work is the 2-week validation sprint in [docs/VALIDATION_SPRINT.md](./docs/VALIDATION_SPRINT.md).

This doc preserves the AI-builder wedge as Pivot 2 if ADHD-Mac validation fails.

---

## Pivot 2 Hypothesis

> **Focusly for AI-native solo builders:** a Mac focus tool for founders and developers who work in Cursor, Claude Code, terminals, docs, and browsers, but lose shipping momentum to context switching.

This is narrower than "ADHD productivity" and may fit founder distribution better.

---

## When Pivot 2 Activates

Activate Pivot 2 if the ADHD-Mac validation sprint fails because:

- fewer than 200 qualified signups and fewer than 20 paid/beta commitments,
- target users reject opt-in drift detection,
- direct competitors are considered good enough,
- the ADHD framing creates too much trust/App Store risk,
- founder/operator interviews show stronger pain and willingness to pay than the primary ADHD-Mac audience.

---

## Builder Wedge To Test

Audience:

- solo founders,
- indie hackers,
- AI-native developers,
- consultants shipping client work,
- people already paying for Cursor/Claude/Linear/Notion.

Problem:

- starts day with a shipping goal,
- drifts into X, Slack, docs, side quests,
- ends day with less shipped than planned,
- needs a lightweight local nudge, not a productivity system.

Product promise:

> **A Mac focus anchor for shipping sessions. It keeps the task visible and nudges you back when you drift from the toolchain you meant to use.**

---

## Builder Validation Gates

Do not build this pivot without validation.

Minimum gates:

- 20 builder interviews,
- 20 paid/beta commitments at $10-$20/mo or equivalent lifetime price,
- 70% of users willing to share active app/window metadata locally,
- clear preference over generic blockers/timers.

---

## Shared Assets With ADHD-Mac Hypothesis

If the validation prototype is built well, these pieces can transfer:

- native Mac task anchor,
- session timer,
- drift nudge mechanism,
- privacy controls,
- re-entry note,
- local history.

Avoid building audience-specific features until one wedge proves demand.
